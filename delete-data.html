What Data Exists

Block Frenzy stores data in two places: locally on your device and in the cloud (if you've used cloud sync, leaderboards, or signed in). Here's what exists and how to delete each type.

📱 Local Data
Game progress, high scores, settings, preferences, profile, encrypted auth tokens
Uninstall app or clear storage
☁️ Cloud Profile

Email us or in-app deletion
🏆 Leaderboard Entries
Your scores and gamer tag on public leaderboards
Email us for removal
📸 AI Avatar
Generated avatar image stored in Cloud Storage (Pro only)
Email us or in-app deletion
📱

Delete Local Data

All local data is stored on your device and is fully under your control. To delete it:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps (or Application Manager)
  3. Find and select Block Frenzy
  4. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage" to erase all app data
  5. Alternatively, uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data
Important

Once deleted, local data cannot be recovered — there is no server-side backup of your local game state unless you have cloud sync enabled.

☁️

Delete Cloud Data

If you have signed in or used cloud sync, some data is stored in our Firebase infrastructure. To request complete cloud data deletion:

Request Deletion

Send an email to support@blockfrenzy.app with the subject line "Delete My Data". Include any identifying information (your display name or the email address linked to your account) so we can locate your records.

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your Firebase Auth account
  • All Firestore profile and sync documents
  • All leaderboard entries
  • Any generated avatar images in Cloud Storage
  • RevenueCat subscriber record (anonymised)

Deletion is completed within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
