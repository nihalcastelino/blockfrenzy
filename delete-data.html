Delete Your Data

Block Frenzy · Last updated 30 March 2026

No account to delete

Block Frenzy does not require an account. There is no registration, no login, and no user profile. We do not store any personal information on our servers.

All data is stored on your device

Your game progress, high scores, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any external server.

How to delete your data

To permanently delete all Block Frenzy data from your device:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Navigate to Apps (or Application Manager)
  3. Find and select Block Frenzy
  4. Tap "Clear Data" or "Clear Storage" to erase all app data
  5. Alternatively, uninstalling the app will permanently delete all associated data

Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered as no backup exists on any server.

Third-party data

Block Frenzy uses Google Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob, which may have collected anonymous device-level data. To manage or delete data held by these services:

Google: visit myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy to manage your Google data and ad personalisation preferences.

Need help?

If you have questions about your data or need assistance, contact us at support@blockfrenzy.app.
