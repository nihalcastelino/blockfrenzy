Privacy Policy

Block Frenzy · Last updated 30 March 2026

Overview

Block Frenzy ("the app") is a mobile puzzle game available on iOS and Android. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data we collect

The app collects anonymous usage analytics via Google Firebase Analytics, including session duration, number of games played, and feature usage. A device advertising identifier may be collected by Google AdMob for the purpose of serving and personalising advertisements. No personally identifiable information — such as your name, email address, phone number, or location — is collected, stored, or transmitted by the app.

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to serve interstitial and rewarded video advertisements. Ads are displayed only between games and are never shown during active gameplay. AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to deliver personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases, including an option to remove interstitial advertisements. All purchases are processed and managed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). The app does not collect, process, or store any payment or financial information.

Data storage

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. No game data is transmitted to external servers. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Third-party services

The app integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

Google Firebase Analytics (privacy policy), Google AdMob (privacy policy), Apple Game Center (privacy policy), Google Play Games Services (privacy policy).

Children's privacy

Block Frenzy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties. Anonymous analytics data is shared only with Google Firebase for the purpose of improving the app experience. Advertising data is shared with Google AdMob solely for the purpose of ad delivery.

Data retention

Analytics data collected by Firebase is retained according to Google's standard data retention policies. The app itself does not maintain any server-side data store. All user data exists only on the user's device.

Your rights

You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalised ads at any time through your device settings. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns, contact us at support@blockfrenzy.app.
